Homemade Ice Cream

Prep Time: 20 minutes Freeze Time: 4 hours Serving: 6
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Chocolate chips, fruits, or toppings
  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together cream, milk, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn until thick.
  3. If you donâ€™t have an ice cream maker, place mixture in freezer and stir every 30 mins.
  4. Once thickened, transfer to an airtight container.
  5. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  6. Scoop into bowls, add toppings, and serve.
